  1. Veg

    • Veg pizza



      Ingredient:

      • 1 pizza base
      • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
      • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
      • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers (red, green, yellow)
      • 1/2 cup sliced onions
      • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
      • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
      • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
      • 1/4 cup sweet corn kernels
      • 1 tsp dried oregano
      • 1 tsp dried basil
      • Salt and black pepper, to taste

      Cooking Step:

      1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
      2. Prepare the pizza base:
        • Place the pizza base on a baking tray or pizza stone.
        • Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base.
      3. Add the toppings:
        • Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
        • Arrange the bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, cherry tomatoes, and corn kernels evenly over the base.
        • Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
      4. Season the pizza:
        • Sprinkle dried oregano and basil over the toppings.
        • Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
      5. Bake the pizza:
        • Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
      6. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.


    • Shahi paneer



      Ingredient

      • 200g paneer, cubed
      • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
      • 1 large onion, finely chopped
      • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
      • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
      • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked and ground to a paste
      • 1/4 cup fresh cream
      • 1/2 cup milk
      • 1 teaspoon garam masala
      • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
      • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
      • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
      • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
      • 2-3 green cardamom pods
      • 1-inch cinnamon stick
      • Salt to taste
      • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

      Cooking Step:

      1. Heat ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat.
      2. Add cumin seeds, cardamom pods, and cinnamon stick. Sauté until fragrant.
      3. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
      4. Mix in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
      5. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
      6. Stir in the cashew nut paste and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
      7. Season the mixture:
        • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
        • Add milk and bring the mixture to a simmer.
      8. Add the paneer cubes and cook for 5-7 minutes, ensuring the paneer is well coated with the gravy.
      9. Pour in the fresh cream and garam masala, mixing well.
      10. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes, then turn off the heat.
      11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.


  2. Non-Veg

    • Mutton curry



      Ingredient:

      • 1 kg mutton, cut into pieces
      • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
      • 2 large onions, finely chopped
      • 2 tomatoes, chopped
      • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
      • 1 cup yogurt
      • 2-3 green chilies, slit
      • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
      • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
      • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
      • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
      • 1 teaspoon garam masala
      • 1 teaspoon whole cumin seeds
      • 4-5 green cardamom pods
      • 2-3 cloves
      • 1 cinnamon stick
      • Salt to taste
      • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

      Cooking Step:

      1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
      2. Add cumin seeds, green cardamom pods, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Sauté until fragrant.
      3. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
      4. Mix in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
      5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
      6. Add the spices:
        • Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well.
      7. Add the mutton pieces to the pot, stirring well to coat with the spice mixture. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the mutton starts to brown.
      8. Add yogurt and slit green chilies. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.
      9. Add water:
        • Pour in enough water to cover the mutton. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the mutton is tender and the gravy has thickened.
      10. Finish the curry:
        • Stir in the garam masala and cook for another 5 minutes.
      11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.


    • Tandoori Chicken


      Ingredient:

      • 4 chicken drumsticks
      • 1 cup yogurt
      • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
      • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
      • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
      • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
      • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
      • 1 teaspoon garam masala
      • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
      • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
      • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
      • Salt to taste
      • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
      • Lemon wedges for serving

      Cooking Step:

      1. Prepare the chicken:
        • Make several deep cuts into the chicken drumsticks to allow the marinade to penetrate.
      2. Make the marinade:
        • In a large bowl, mix together the yogurt, lemon juice, vegetable oil, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground black pepper, and salt.
      3. Marinate the chicken:
        • Add the chicken drumsticks to the marinade, making sure they are well coated.
        • Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
      4. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
      5. Cook the chicken:
        • Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray lined with aluminum foil or parchment paper.
        • Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred on the edges.
      6. Garnish and serve:
        • Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes.
        • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with lemon wedges.
